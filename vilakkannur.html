Vilakkannur, 2013 (Recognized 2025)

← Back to Miracles

The Eucharistic miracle of Vilakkannur occurred on November 15, 2013, at Christ the King Parish, Kannur district, Kerala. During Holy Mass, the distinct holy face of Jesus appeared on a consecrated Host, leading to its official Vatican recognition in 2025 after rigorous scientific validation.

Scientific Validation

In 2024, the Host underwent advanced testing at Christ University, Bangalore, using FTIR, HPLC, and Mass Spectrometry. These tests confirmed that the image was not artificially applied, as its chemical composition perfectly matched the wheat-based structure of the Host, ruling out any form of human tampering.

Vatican Recognition

Following a comprehensive investigative report submitted to Rome in April 2024, the Congregation for the Doctrine of the Faith officially recognized the event in March 2025. It was publicly proclaimed on May 31, 2025, as India’s first Vatican-recognized Eucharistic miracle.
