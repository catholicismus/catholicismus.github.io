Legnica, 2013

← Back to Miracles

The 2013 Eucharistic miracle of Legnica, Poland, occurred at St. Hyacinth’s Shrine when a dropped consecrated Host transformed into human heart tissue, confirmed through rigorous scientific analysis.

The Event

On Christmas Day 2013, a Host fell to the floor and was placed in water to dissolve. Instead, it developed red stains. Histopathological studies by independent universities later identified the substance as fragments of human myocardial (heart) tissue in a state of intense agony.

Church Approval

After reviewing the scientific dossier, the Vatican’s Congregation for the Doctrine of the Faith and local Bishop Zbigniew Kiernikowski officially recognized the event in 2016 as having the "hallmarks of a supernatural event." Today, the miraculous Host is exposed for adoration, serving as a powerful testament to the Real Presence of Christ.
