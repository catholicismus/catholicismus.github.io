Sokółka, 2008

← Back to Miracles

The 2008 Eucharistic miracle of Sokółka, Poland, occurred when a consecrated Host, placed in water to dissolve, transformed into living human heart tissue, as confirmed by independent histopathological examination.

The Scientific Evidence

Independent tests conducted by Dr. Maria Sobaniec-Łotowska and Dr. Stanisław Sulkowski revealed critical findings:

  • Human Myocardium: The sample was identified as living cardiac muscle tissue.
  • Signs of Agony: The tissue showed structural alterations characteristic of a heart undergoing the final spasms before death.
  • Inseparable Fusion: Under an electron microscope, the filaments of the eucharistic bread and human heart tissue were intricately interwoven—a structural fusion that scientists stated could not be replicated by human intervention.

Church Status

Following a thorough investigation, the Metropolitan Curia of Białystok confirmed the event's authenticity. The miraculous Host is now enshrined for public veneration, drawing countless pilgrims who seek to witness this profound testament to the Real Presence of Christ.
