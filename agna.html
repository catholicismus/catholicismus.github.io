Sequence of Events (July - August 2024)

In mid-2024, a series of seven reported supernatural incidents centered around Agna, a 10th-grade student in Kochi, Kerala, caught the attention of the local faithful. Five of these incidents involved the physical transformation of the Holy Eucharist.

The Eucharistic Transformations

  • The First Incident: The initial transformation occurred during a standard Holy Mass, leaving the student frightened and confused.
  • The Second Incident (July 22): Preceded by vivid dreams, the Host transformed on her tongue and was preserved by Fr. Sebastian Vattaparambil.
  • The Third Incident (July 29): The most widely documented event, where the Host reportedly turned into a dense, heart-shaped lump of bleeding flesh.
  • The Fourth Incident: Occurred during a school Mass, witnessed by students and teachers.
  • The Fifth Incident (August 4): The final bleeding Host, witnessed by parish staff and the pastoral committee.

Reflections on Faith

This series of events reminds us of the profound mystery of the Real Presence. While the Church remains deliberate in its process of investigation—balancing reports of supernatural phenomena with careful canonical review—the devotion these events have inspired among the local community remains a testament to the importance of the Eucharist in daily life.
