Montserrat, 1657

← Back to Miracles

The 1657 Eucharistic miracle of Montserrat, Spain, involves the liberation of a soul from purgatory through the offering of three consecutive Masses, witnessed by a young girl whose visions were verified by the monks.

The Vision of Liberation

A young girl requested three Masses for her deceased father at the Monastery of Our Lady of Montserrat. During the first Mass, she saw him in fire; by the second, he appeared transformed and joyous; during the third, she witnessed his soul ascending to heaven. The supernatural reality of these visions was confirmed when a tissue placed near the invisible flames of purgatory during the first Mass caught fire instantly.

Legacy

This event, documented by Benedictine priest R.P. Francio de Paula Crusellas, stands as a unique testimony to the efficacy of the Holy Mass for the faithful departed. It remains a cherished part of the records compiled by Blessed Carlo Acutis in his international exhibition on Eucharistic miracles.
